<p>
  The right side of the heart receives <b>deoxygenated</b> blood and sends it to the lungs. 
  In the <b>lungs</b>, O<sub>2</sub> is added to the blood. 
  The left side of the heart receives <b>oxygenated</b> blood from the lungs and sends it to the rest of the body. 
  There are various cardiac variables that are influenced by the heart. 
  A few of them can be found here.
</p>


<p>
The heart's function can be measured through various variables that indicate just how well it is functioning. Every aspect of the heart's anatomy and function works together to pump our blood through our bodies. The <b>cardiac variables</b> indicate how well our heart is functioning. Variations in <a href="cardiac_variables.md">cardiac variables</a> can be seen during exercise,at rest and even in differing health conditions. These variables allow us to better understand just how our heart alters its function to support our bodies needs.
